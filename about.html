About Us

What we do

Dry Stone Walling is an acient art, with some walls lasting for millennia. A typical life span for a dry stone wall is well over a century, however it takes a skilled craftman to be able to achieve this. We have over half a century of experience dedicated to the craft and work closely with clients to ensure we deliver. Each project begins with sourcing the perfect stone for the job. Where possible we aim to cherry pick the best stone from local quarries, but will any distance to get the right stone for you. Different types of stone can be used in drystone walling, from limestone, sandstone to granite, this allows us to create the perfect wall in various styles for an situation. The 2 main types of dry stone wall are traditional & garden walls.

Both syles of walls can be used as:

  • Field Walls
  • Retaining Walls
  • Boundary Walls

Traditional Walls

One of our traditon walls here, which was used to surround a stunning garden. Traditonal Dry Stone Walls are the ones you typically see surrounding farm fields in the counrty side. These have the rustic charm most associated with dry stone walls.
